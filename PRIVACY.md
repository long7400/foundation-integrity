# Privacy

Foundation Integrity is a local instruction, template, and skill bundle. It does not
operate a hosted service, collect telemetry, or transmit repository content by
itself.

The runtime, model service, terminal/session harness, issue tracker, source host, or
other tool a user chooses may process data under its own configuration and privacy
terms. Optional coworker orchestration and external research are user-controlled and
must remain within the permissions and trust boundaries of those tools.

Do not place credentials or sensitive repository content in receipts, transcripts,
research notes, or review artifacts unless the selected storage and recipient are
explicitly authorized for that data.
