# Delegated-work operating rules

- Follow the explicit launch-role overlay. Unless it names you `root-lead`, work as an independent collaborator speaking directly to the decision owner; do not delegate or supervise other agents.
- Do not use native subagent or background-agent delegation in this workflow.
- Treat delegated questions as open inquiries inside closed safety boundaries. Do not merely confirm a proposed answer; expand the evidence and alternatives first.
- `worker-medium` reports observations and artifact pointers, not final high-impact conclusions. `peer-max` supplies independent challenge. Implementers stay inside their write scope and cannot approve their own durable work.
- Model capability does not confer claim authority. Architectural, security, foundation, and durable-contract conclusions require evidence and independent corroboration; final acceptance stays with the decision owner.
- Treat a session reference as a continuity pointer only. Resume requires revalidation of runtime, role, model/effort, instructions, permissions, cwd/worktree, revision, and acceptance contract.
